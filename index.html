Why Bold Impact

We believe trust should be the default in giving. That’s why we verify cases, transfer funds directly, and publish redacted receipts for the public.

  • Verification first: Cases are screened and prioritized.
  • Direct help: Disbursements go to recipients or vendors.
  • Proof you can see: Receipts are posted after payout.

Disbursement timelines

  • 3–7 business days post verification
  • 24–72 hours for urgent medical cases
Rollover policy

FAQs

Is ₹1 really okay?

Yes. Micro-giving adds up fast and keeps help flowing consistently.

Can I choose where my donation goes?

Yes. Pick a cause when you donate. If a case can’t proceed, we reassign transparently or refund on request before disbursement.

How will I know my donation helped?

We publish redacted receipts and case updates in the payout tracker.

✦ FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is ₹1 really okay?
Yes — absolutely. Micro-giving adds up fast. Even ₹1 from many donors can cover an entire case. The ₹1 minimum exists because we want everyone to be able to participate, regardless of what they can spare.
Can I choose where my donation goes?
Yes — pick a cause category (Medical, Education, Relief, or Where needed most) when you donate. If a matching case can't proceed, we'll reassign your donation transparently or refund it on request before disbursement.
How will I know my donation helped?
Redacted receipts and case outcomes appear in the Transparency tracker. If you shared your email, we'll also send a personal update when funds from your donation pool are disbursed.
Can I get a refund?
Yes — if a case can't proceed and you'd prefer a refund over reassignment, email contact.craftysatisfaction@gmail.com before disbursement and we'll process it promptly.
Is my personal information safe?
Yes. Donor details are stored locally in your browser only. Public receipts are always redacted — no personal information is ever published. Your email is only used to send you case updates if you opt in.
